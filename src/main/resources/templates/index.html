The Haunted Docks

The old docks lay in ruins where once there was a bustling community of tradeships and sea fairers plying the waters in search of riches. No one is sure what happened, but one day several very powerful witches showed up, and everyone fled. It has since become a place of extreme danger where only the most capable travels dare to go.
